### Permittivity and permeability

$\varepsilon_0$: permittivity of free space, $\varepsilon=\varepsilon_r\varepsilon_0$: permittivity in dielectric
$\mu_0$: permeability of free space, $\mu=\mu_r\mu_0$: permittivity in dielectric

Note: 
- $\mu_{r}\approx 1$ for all dielectrics expect for ferromagnetic ones
- $c=\frac{1}{\sqrt{\varepsilon_0\mu_{0}}}$ is the speed of light in free space, $v=\frac{1}{\sqrt{\varepsilon_r\varepsilon_0\mu_{r}\mu_{0}}}=\frac{c}{\sqrt{\varepsilon_{r}\mu_{r}}}\approx\frac{c}{\sqrt{\varepsilon_{r}}}$ is the speed of light in a dielectric medium.
- $n\equiv \frac{c}{v}=\sqrt{\varepsilon_r}$ is called **refraction index**.
