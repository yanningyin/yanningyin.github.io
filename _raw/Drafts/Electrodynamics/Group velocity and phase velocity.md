Ref: [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Group_velocity#:~:text=The%20group%20velocity%20of%20a,the%20wave%E2%80%94propagates%20through%20space)

When a wave packet consists of a series of plane waves with different $k$ and $\omega$, each plane wave has its own phase velocity (perhaps there is also an overall phase velocity), and the envelop of the packet has a group velocity.
